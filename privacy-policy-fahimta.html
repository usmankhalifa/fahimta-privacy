Fahimta – Privacy Policy

Effective date: October 01, 2025

Fahimta (“the App”) is an educational mobile application that provides Hausa-language learning and voice features. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

If you do not agree with this Policy, please do not install or use the App.

2. What data we collect

We strive to process the minimum data necessary for the App to work.

2.1 Account & profile (if you sign in)

  • Name, email address, profile photo (if you choose to provide).

2.2 App usage & diagnostics

  • In‑app events (e.g., lesson started/completed), basic device information (model, OS version), crash logs, and performance metrics.
  • Purpose: to improve reliability, measure feature usage, and fix bugs.

2.3 Voice & audio (only when you use voice features)

  • Microphone audio and transcriptions.
  • On‑device vs cloud: Where possible, speech processing is done on the device. If you choose cloud‑based speech recognition, audio snippets may be sent securely to our speech provider solely to return text; we do not use audio to identify you.
  • Retention: We do not keep raw audio after processing. Transcripts may be stored locally on your device to show your history unless you clear them.

2.4 Payments (if applicable)

  • If you purchase content, payment processing is handled by Google Play and/or the selected payment provider. We do not receive your full card details.

3. Why we collect data (legal bases)

  • Provide the service: enable lessons, speech‑to‑text, text‑to‑speech.
  • Improve & secure the App: diagnostics, analytics, and fraud prevention.
  • Comply with law & enforce terms.

4. Sharing your data

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Service providers acting on our instructions (e.g., analytics, crash reporting, optional cloud STT/TTS).
  • Authorities when required by law or to protect users and our rights.

All providers must protect your data and may not use it for their own purposes.

Examples of processors (may vary by build):
Google Play Services, Firebase Crashlytics/Analytics.
Optional speech services (e.g., cloud speech‑to‑text) when you enable cloud mode.

5. International transfers

If we transfer data across borders, we use reasonable safeguards (e.g., standard contractual clauses) where required.

6. Data retention

  • Account/profile: retained while your account is active.
  • Usage & diagnostics: retained for a limited period needed for analysis and troubleshooting.
  • Voice audio: not retained after processing; transcripts may remain on your device until you delete them.

When no longer needed, data is deleted or anonymized.

7. Your choices & rights

  • Permissions: You can revoke Microphone/Storage/Notifications in your device settings. The App may stop working for features that need those permissions.
  • Access & deletion: You can request a copy or deletion of your personal data by emailing us.
  • Opt‑out of analytics: Use the in‑app settings (if available) or your device’s ad/analytics settings.

8. Children’s privacy

The App is designed for general audiences and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If your app targets children or schools, obtain verifiable parental consent where required and provide a supervised experience. If we learn we’ve collected data from a child under 13 without consent, we will delete it.

9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures (encryption in transit, access controls, least‑privilege practices) to protect your data. No system is 100% secure.

10. Third‑party links

The App may link to external sites we don’t control. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

11. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy periodically. We will post the updated version here and update the “Effective date”.
